HOW TO PLAY

Keyboard and Mouse: Click to play, R to reset scores

Controller: D-Pad/Left Stick to navigate, A to select, START to reset

RayTTT is a neon-styled Tic-Tac-Toe game built with C++ and raylib. Smooth animations, bright retro colors, and simple, intuitive controls make it perfect for quick matches — solo or with a friend. Scores are tracked automatically to keep the rivalry alive!
